About Me

My name is Kiley Taylor, or Kai for short, and I am a web developer in training. Currently, outside of school, I am a teacher in early childhood education and an entertainer. After going to school and working in my current field for a number of years, I realized that it wasn't the career path I wanted to be on any longer. So now I'm putting in the work to change my life. Sometimes it's a little scary and it can be a little overwhelming but I'm working on it and I'm excited for the future!

In my spare time, I enjoy listening to podcasts, reading, exercising, and occasionally drawing. My favourite podcasts are Lore, Monsters Among Us, Why Won't You Date Me, and Never Seen It. I love to read manga, short stories, and comics mainly. Recently I've also turned my drawings into digital pieces and I'm working on a side project of digital portraits of all my friends.
